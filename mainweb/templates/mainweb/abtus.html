About Us

Government Polytechnic, Thane.

What we are

Our Vision


Emerging as socially & industrially preferred learning institution & leading social interventions through Technology education for better tomorrow & human & professional values.

Our Mission


This institute is committed to provide diverse, proactive & socially responsive work culture & environment developing technically competent & employable technicians & entrepreneurs through adapting interactive & technology-Lead Teaching learning strategies.

Core Values


  • Accountability & Transparency.
  • Team Spirit.
  • Discipline
  • Environmental Sustainability.
  • Mutual Trust.
  • Equality.
  • Satisfaction of Stakeholders.

Quality Policy


Government Polytechnic, Thane is committed to provide quality technical education to its students who will be acceptable as preferred technicians to economy & society.
The institute strives to achieve its quality goals through implementation of effective Teaching-Learning processes in the institute, continuous up-gradation of its instructional facilities & continual development of its faculty & staff.

We are committed to :


  • Provide learning & Re-skilling opportunities to adult learners through training & continuing education Institutional Infrastructure.
  • Provide fair opportunities to institute employees for their professional growth & career advancement.
  • Provide equitable learning opportunities for women, physically challenged & economically weaker sections of the society.
  • Develop institute itself as knowledge-sink for the small & medium scale industries in the region.
  • Groom the learners as the professional human resources & responsible citizens of India.
  • Encourage entrepreneurial skills in learners.
  • Provide effective guidance & counseling, placement & other student services.
