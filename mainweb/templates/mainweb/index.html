Introduction

Government polytechnic Thane, established by Government of Maharashtra, governed by Higher & Technical Education Department, administered by DTE, approved by AICTE & affiliated to MSBTE is a co-education institute responsible for imparting industry-responsive Diploma level programs in the field of engineering, technology & professional education to the students admitted as per norms stipulated by Government of Maharashtra. It has the onus of bringing in socioeconomic transformations through education, training & linkages with industries & other fraternity, for the development of society at large. It is further responsible to contribute in development of technically trained human resources in Global context.
