Gymkhana Information

The Academic progress of the student is supported by extracurricular activities through the framework of Gymkhana. It provides a platform to shape & nourish the inner qualities of the students. Gymkhana organizes various activities and sport events for overall development of the students. Gymkhana also provides various well developed indoor & outdoor facilities.
